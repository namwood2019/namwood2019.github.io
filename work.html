Wood Database

A gateway to provide and make accessible information about wood species. In addition to the basic informations such as where they grow, general description, and abundance, each wood species profile includes working and physical properties, availability, strength and mechanical properties, and main uses.

Wood Identification

This web-based appliation has been developed for identifying woods. While other tools have been developed for identifying woods, they often require extensive scientific knowledge or complexe technologies that are often unavailable to the personnel involved. In contrast, this tool is a simple that can be used by anyone.

Wood Laboratory

The remote wood laboratory will be a digital platform, where students can study from home. In virtual wood laboratory, students can perform experiments and deliver authentic results online for a number of teaching modules in the field of wood science and technology such as wood anatomy, properties, conversion and uses.
