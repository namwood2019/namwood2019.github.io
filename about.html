About Me

I am Quy Nam Nguyen, a web developer and wood scientist.

My background is in wood science, a brief career teaching wood science at the university level, and over 10 years working in wood laboratories.
Beside the teaching and research activities, I have been actively involved in serving the wood industries and organizations to meet their demands in wood identification, and testing of wood and related products. Working in wood laboratories for years, I realized the need of web applications in developing the wood databases and wood solutions. I has started and never stopped coding since then.

I love wood and I love coding!.
